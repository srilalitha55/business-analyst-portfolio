📘 Sales & Finance Analytics Project

A complete analysis of customer performance, market growth, and financial health using Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX.

📊 Sales Report
🎯 Project Objectives

Develop a detailed Customer Performance Report to understand customer contribution and revenue behavior.

Compare Market Performance vs. Sales Targets to evaluate how different regions are performing.

🔍 Why Sales Analytics is Important

Sales analytics enables businesses to:

Track sales activities in real time

Identify buying patterns

Measure and monitor KPIs

📈 Business Impact

Helps define smart discount strategies

Supports strong data-backed negotiations

Identifies potential markets with high growth opportunities

💰 Finance Report
🎯 Project Objectives

Build Profit & Loss (P&L) reports by Fiscal Year and Month

Create P&L reports segmented by Market for regional financial comparison

🔍 Why Finance Analytics Matters

Supports managerial decision-making

Helps benchmark financial performance

Forms the base for budgeting and forecasting

📈 Value Added

Aligns financial planning with strategic organizational goals

Builds confidence in the company’s financial outlook

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

Excel – Data cleaning, modeling, and report creation

Power Query – ETL, generating date tables, fiscal logic

Power Pivot – Data modeling, relationships, and measures

DAX – Creating calculated columns and KPIs

🧠 Technical Skills Demonstrated

ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) methodology

Building custom date tables

Creating fiscal months & fiscal quarters

Establishing relationships in data models

Integrating multiple datasets into one unified model

Designing dynamic reports using DAX

🤝 Soft Skills Showcased

Strong understanding of sales & finance reporting

User-first approach to dashboard and report design

Optimizing reports for better performance

Structured planning and execution of report development
